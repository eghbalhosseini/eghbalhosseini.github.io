\noindent
Research at the intersection of computational neuroscience and AI, studying how data and context shape internal representations in Large Language Models. Prior work covers representational structure during pretraining and in-context learning, and convergence across modalities and the brain. Current focus: pre- and post-training strategies for agentic behavior and long-term planning.
