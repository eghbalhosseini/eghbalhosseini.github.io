Eghbal Hosseini

My name is Eghbal and I am a graduate student in the lab of Evelina Fedorenko studying neural basis of language comprehension. Previously I was a researcher at Jazayer lab at MIT, where I worked on neural basis of timing and variabiability. Before that I worked with Wilsaan Joiner on sensorimotor learning. I got my bachelors degree in Electrical Egineering from Iran University of Science and Tech, Tehran, Iran. Creative Commons.
